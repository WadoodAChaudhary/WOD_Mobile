Word: كان
Word_ID:364
Transliteration:kaAna
MeaningSimple: is, has been, will be, become.
Meaning:  كَانَ is used in the sense of “is”, denoting everlastingness or continuity.
Example:  Token(33:52:32:35) Allah is All-Knowing Wise (33:52).
Meaning: كَانَ is used in the sense of “was".
Example: Token(16:121:1:5): Abraham was a paragon of virtue (16:121).
Meaning:  كَانَ in the sense of "will be".
Example: Token(76:8:5:7): The evil of it will be widespread (76:8).
Meaning:  كَانَ in the sense of "became".  
Example: Token(2:35:11:13): He became of the disbelievers(2:35).
Meaning:   كَانَ in the sense of "it behoves”. 
Example: Token(3:80:1:3): It does not behove a man (3:80).
Meaning:  كَانَ in the sense of "laying stress or emphasis".  For  كَوَنَ see مَكُنَ
Meaning: كَانَ [aor.  يَكِيْنُ inf. noun كَيْنًا ] كَانَ بِفُلانٍ  : He humbled himself before such a one. كَانَ وَاسْتَكَانَ  are syn.
Example: وَمَا اسْتَكَانُوْا : And they did not humble themselves (before the enemy) (3:147).
