<Text>كان</Text>
<Status>NEW</Status>
<Word_ID>364</Word_ID>
<MeaningSimple>is, has been, will be, become.</MeaningSimple>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ is used in the sense of “is”, denoting everlastingness or continuity.</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
		<Example>Token(33:52:32:35) Allah is All-Knowing Wise (33:52).</Example>
	</Meaning>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ is used in the sense of “was".</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
		<Example>Token(16:121:1:5): Abraham was a paragon of virtue (16:121).</Example>
	</Meaning>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ in the sense of "will be".</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
		<Example>Token(76:8:5:7): The evil of it will be widespread (76:8).</Example>
	</Meaning>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ in the sense of "became".</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
		<Example>Token(2:35:11:13): He became of the disbelievers(2:35).</Example>
	</Meaning>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ in the sense of "it behoves”.</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
		<Example>Token(3:80:1:3): It does not behove a man (3:80).</Example>
	</Meaning>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ in the sense of "laying stress or emphasis".  For  كَوَنَ see مَكُنَ</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
	</Meaning>
	<Meaning>
		<Definition>كَانَ [aor.  يَكِيْنُ inf. noun كَيْنًا ] كَانَ بِفُلانٍ  : He humbled himself before such a one. كَانَ وَاسْتَكَانَ  are syn.</Definition>
		<Reference>Ghulam Farid</Reference>
		<Example>وَمَا اسْتَكَانُوْا : And they did not humble themselves (before the enemy) (3:147).</Example>
	</Meaning>
</Word>
