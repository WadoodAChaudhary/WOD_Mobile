 Word:كرب
Root:To grieve, afflict, overburden, distress, twist a rope, tighten.
Transliteration:karob
MeaningSimple:Affliction, distress, pain
Meaning: كَرَبَ  aor.  يَكْرُبُ inf. noun  كَرْبًا .  كَرَبَ الْحَبْلَ  : He twisted the rope. كَرَبَ الْقَيْدَ عَلَى الْمُقَيَّدِ  : He straitened the shackles upon the shackled. كَرَبَهُ الْغَمُّ  : The grief afflicted, distressed or oppressed him.  كَرْبٌ  : Grief or distress that affects the breath; anxiety or disquietude of the mind.
Example:Token(21:77:10:12) :From the great distress (21:77).
