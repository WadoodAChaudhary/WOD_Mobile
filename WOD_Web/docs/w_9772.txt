 Word:كاس
Root: A cup for drinking if there is a drink in it. It can also refer to the drink itself, e.g. wine. If there is no beverage in it, the drinking cup is called Qadehun from the root: Qaf-Dal-Ha.
Transliteration:kasin
MeaningSimple:Cup. Goblet.
Meaning: كَاْسٌ [plural كُؤُوْسٌ ] : A drinking cup; a cup full of wine; a goblet: 
Example:Token(37:46:3:5): A full cup from a flowing fountain (37: 46).
