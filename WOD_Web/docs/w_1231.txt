 Word:كرة
Root:To return to, return successively, run against, repeat, come back, follow by turns, be wound (thread).
Transliteration:kara
MeaningSimple: To turn against. Return. Prevail after losing. Second chance
Meaning:كَرَّ aor.  يَكُرُّ inf. noun كَرًّا and  كُرُوْرًا and تَكْرَارًا : He turned to or against him. اِنْهَزَمَ ثُمَّ كَرَّ عَلَيْهِ  : He was put to flight, then he returned against him; he gave up his ghost.  كَرَّةٌ  : A return.
Example:Token(2:168:5:7) : Would that there were for us a return (2:168); a time or one time.
Example:Token(67:5:1:4) كَرَّتَيْنِ  : Again (67:5); a turn to prevail against an opposing party.
Example:Token(17:7:1:4)  Then We gave to you the turn to prevail against them or victory over them (17:7).
