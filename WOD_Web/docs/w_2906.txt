Word:كثر
Root:To surpass in number or quantity, increase, multiply, something that happens often; many, numerous.
Transliteration:katheer
MeaningSimple:Numerous, copious.
Meaning: كَثُرَ     aor.  يَكْثُرُ and  يَكْثِرُ inf. noun كَثْرَةٌ  : It was or became much,copious, many, numerous; it multiplied. كَثَرُوْا عَلَيْهِ فَغَلَبُوْهُ  : They multiplied against him and overcame him.
Example:Token(4:8:13:17)   Whether it be little or much (4:8).
Example:Token(7:87:1:3)  فَكَثَّرَكُمْ   : So He multiplied you (7:87).
Example:Token(89:13:1:2)  And they wrought much  corruption therein (89:13).
Meaning:تَكَاثَرُوْا  : They contended one with another for superiority in number. اَلتَّكَاثُرُ  : Signifies the contending together for superiority in amount or number of property and children and men (102:2).
Example:Token(102:2:1:2) 
Meaning: اِسْتَكْثَرَ مِنَ الشَّىْءِ  : He desired or wished or sought for much of a thing; he reckoned it much or many.
Example:Token(7:189:1:3)  He should have secured abundance of good (7:189).
Meaning: كَثْرَةٌ  : Abundance or numerousness. 
Example: كَثِيْرٌ  : Many or much. (2:110)
Meaning: اَكْثَرَ  : Comparative degree of كَثِيْرٌ as  كَوْثَرٌ  : A lord or master; abounding in good; a man possessing much good and who gives much (108:2).
